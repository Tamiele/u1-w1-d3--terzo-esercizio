Focaccia whith Caramelized Onions, cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds whith this festive focaccia appetizer topped whith sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and cream brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredient

For the Focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vineagar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind of whith a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
